title: When to see a professional
source: general wellbeing guidance (original summary)

This tool offers wellbeing context only and never diagnoses. If a signal stays clearly outside your usual range across several days, if you feel genuinely unwell, or if you are ever worried, the right step is to speak with a qualified professional — a doctor, nurse, or relevant specialist. Trust how you feel over any number. Seeking advice early is sensible, and this signpost is exactly that: a prompt to talk to a person, never a conclusion about what is happening.
